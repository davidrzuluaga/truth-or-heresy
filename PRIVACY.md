# Privacy Policy — Truth or Heresy

_Last updated: 2026-04-23_

Truth or Heresy ("the App") is developed and operated by the app publisher
("we", "us"). This policy explains what information the App handles and how.

## The short version

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** Truth or Heresy
is a single-player trivia game. Everything you do in the App — your score,
streak, XP, unlocked badges, progress through mastery paths, review queue —
is stored **only on your device**. No analytics, no ad tracking, no accounts.

## What's stored on your device

The following is saved locally using your device's standard app storage:

- Score, streak, XP, level, badges
- Mastery-path progress and spaced-repetition review queue
- Hearts remaining and the timestamp of your last hearts reset
- A flag indicating whether you've unlocked Premium
- A flag indicating whether you've seen the first-launch paywall preview

This data is removed when you delete the App from your device.

## In-app purchases

If you purchase Premium, the transaction is processed by Apple (App Store) or
Google (Play Store). We receive a receipt confirming the purchase and, via
RevenueCat (our billing infrastructure provider), the purchase entitlement is
associated with your device's anonymous app-user ID. We do not see your
payment details, name, or email — those remain with Apple or Google.

RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/

## Analytics & advertising

The App contains **no third-party analytics** and **no advertising**.

## Children

The App does not target children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data
from them. Since we collect no personal data, this is not a concern in
practice.

## Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, if
the change is material, prompt you on next launch.
